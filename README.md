# About Startup Saga 5.0

Startup Saga is the flagship entrepreneurship event organized annually by the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Cell at Army Institute of Technology (AIT). The event is dedicated to fostering the entrepreneurial spirit and creating a robust startup ecosystem, aligning with the national vision of building a $5 trillion economy. Startup Saga serves as a premier platform allowing aspiring entrepreneurs, primarily students, to develop innovative business models, connect with expert mentors, engage renowned investors, and gain real-world exposure.

## Our Mission

We aim to nurture startup talent within AIT and beyond by providing an ecosystem where ideas can transform into viable startups. Our efforts have contributed to successful ventures like FreightFox, Bluebricks, Udchalo, and many more. Startup Saga continuously supports promising startups by offering strategic guidance and funding opportunities to accelerate their growth.

## Key Highlights

- Over 5,000+ registrations and 3,000+ active attendees from various colleges, industries, and professional backgrounds.
- Interaction with senior industry leaders, angel investors, venture capitalists, and successful alumni entrepreneurs.
- Prize pool exceeding ₹75 Lakhs to fuel startup innovations and commercial expansion.

## Major Events

### Startup Expo
The central event of Startup Saga, Startup Expo connects startups with investors and customers. It offers pre-analyzed pitch decks, fully equipped stalls, and a platform to amplify visibility. Opportunities include impressing top investors, securing funding up to ₹50 Lakhs, and networking with over 4,000 attendees, including industry experts and media.

### NextNiche
An 18-hour intensive business hackathon focused on high-impact sectors such as defense, healthcare, edtech, AI, logistics, and cybersecurity. Participants form teams to build innovative, scalable solutions judged on innovation, feasibility, and market approach.

### Shark Tank
Participants pitch their business ideas to a panel of distinguished judges, simulating the popular innovation-show format. Winning startups receive mentorship and investment offers, potentially gaining a launchpad for immediate growth.

### E-Cell Meet
A highly interactive event bringing together E-cell representatives from various colleges. The meet fosters collaboration, sharing of best practices, and strategic partnerships aimed at strengthening the student startup ecosystem.

## Minor Events & Engagements

To complement the core events, Startup Saga hosts several activities designed to sharpen entrepreneurial skills and foster networking in an engaging environment:

- **Business Knowledge Games:** These include interactive quizzes like “2 Truths - 1 Lie” and “Meme Mania” that challenge business knowledge in a fun, competitive format.
- **Stand-Up Comedy Show:** Featuring a professional comedian, this show blends humor with real-life entrepreneurial stories and insights connecting engineering and corporate worlds.

## Why Participate?

- **For Startups:** Gain exclusive access to funding, mentorship, expert feedback, visibility, and a thriving entrepreneurial network.
- **For Investors:** Discover promising early-stage ventures, participate in exclusive investment opportunities, and build valuable industry connections.
- **For Students and Attendees:** Engage with industry leaders, learn from experienced entrepreneurs, and network with peers passionate about innovation.
